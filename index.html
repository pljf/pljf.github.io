Computer Science and Mathematics student at Hunter College, CUNY.

I'm Patrick Luo, a student based in New York and graduating in May 2027. My work lives at the intersection of full-stack software, artificial intelligence, and market-focused analysis. I like projects where the interface, data flow, and decision-making logic all have to line up cleanly.

Outside the code itself, I enjoy learning from ambitious technical problems, collaborating with people who care about craft, and improving the way I communicate ideas. My long-term goal is to keep growing as an engineer who can move between product, data, AI, and finance without losing attention to detail.
